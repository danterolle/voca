# Benchmark (EN → 24 languages)

Run on Apple M2 (16 GB RAM). Times are per-sentence averages across 3 sentences.

Measured end-to-end through Loqi's pipeline (`commands.SetupRun` → `core.Translate`), which includes config loading, Ollama connectivity check, prompt construction, and HTTP call setup.

> **S1 (Booking):** "I would like to book a table for two at seven o'clock this evening, preferably near the window with a view of the garden."
>
> **S2 (Tech):** "The conference will cover topics such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data privacy regulations across different countries."
>
> **S3 (Culture):** "The museum's new exhibition features over two hundred paintings and sculptures from Renaissance artists, attracting visitors from all over the world."

## Comparison

| Model | Params | Size | Avg time | Quality |
|-------|--------|------|----------|---------|
| Gemma 4 E2B QAT | 4.6B | 4.3 GB | ~11.4s | Best — grammatically correct across all 24 langs, natural phrasing |
| Phi-4 Mini | 3.8B | 2.5 GB | ~1.4s | Solid EU coverage, degrades on Arabic/Thai, occasional placeholder translation glitches |

Collected June 2026 (re-run 21 June 2026).
